About Me

Proud father of 2 boys, and happily married. Living in Manor, TX. After quiet a bit of moving around and finally settling here in Texas. I am a Navy Veteran, serving during Operation Iraqi Freedeom, and Operation Enduring Freedom. After some job changes I decided to take advantage of my military benefits and take a certification course for Full Stack Web Development. My personal strengths currently are responsive layouts using Bootstrap and similar libraries, HTML5, CSS3, and some javascript. I have an entry-level knowledge of React.js, Node.js and basic understanding of the use of Mongo, MySql.
